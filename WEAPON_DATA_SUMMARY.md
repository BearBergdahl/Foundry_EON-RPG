## Data Structure

Each weapon entry includes:
- **namn** (name): Display name in Swedish
- **grupp** (group): Weapon category (dolk or svard)
- **enhand** (one-hand): Usage stats with tvarde (dice) and bonus
- **tvahand** (two-hand): Usage stats with tvarde and bonus
- **hugg** (slash): Damage type with tvarde and bonus
- **kross** (crush): Damage type with tvarde and bonus
- **stick** (pierce): Damage type with tvarde and bonus
- **langd** (length): Weapon length in cm
- **vikt** (weight): Weapon weight in kg
- **egenskaper** (properties): Array of special properties with optional values
